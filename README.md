### My Portfolio Website

The purpose of this website is to not only showcase my work, but to eventually share my philosophy on both programming and self-development.
I would like for this project to be ever-growing. A place for experiments with some web tools and for expression of my taste, of ideas about learning and some opinions on new tools.

## Future Plans / TODOs:

- Playing with animation and interactivity (Try the GSAP library?)
- Skills section: detailing the intersection of the different fields I come from or am interested in
- AI Experiments section, separate from current hand-built projects
- Q&A Section: quick thoughts and answers to questions to introduce myself better -> what tools do I use to learn? what is my approach to AI? what are my big goals in the future? etc.
- Adding a Blog section

## Stack

Right now I am building it with React, with no backend since there is no truly dynamic content. As I experiment more with what this site is, it may change!

## Design and Styling

I don't use any templates or premade components (my emphasis is still on learning and practicing) + then I get to experience the magic of designing things in Figma and building them exactly how I want.
For this, Tailwind has been particularly useful to me, speeding up my workflow by quite a bit.
